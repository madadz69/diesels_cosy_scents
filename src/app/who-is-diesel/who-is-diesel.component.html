Meet Diesel

Talvarna Diesel

Diesel is the heart of Diesel's Cosy Scents—a Norwegian Forest Cat with a coat that holds warmth and memories. Every blend we make is inspired by his calm confidence, gentle purrs, and the scents that make him feel safe.

Diesel on a Diesel?

Diesel got his name from my partner (Adam) who has a love of anything cars and motorsport, because Diesel (the cat) is blue and smoke, Adam chose his name because a diesel engine tends to produce that colour!

Forest Explorer

Diesel loves to climb trees, and hide in the undergrowth, often coming home covered in leaves, bark and smelling like the forest!

Cosy Evenings

Life is not all go for Diesel, he also loves relaxing especially in places that remind him of the forest.
