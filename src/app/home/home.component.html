About Us

Welcome to our fragrance sanctuary. Inspired by my beloved cat Diesel, we specialise in creating unique, pet-friendly hand-crafted scents that transform your space into a cozy haven. My passion for a fresh-smelling home, while ensuring Diesel's safety and comfort, led to this collection. Each product is carefully curated and tested to be cat-friendly while bringing warmth and comfort to your home.
